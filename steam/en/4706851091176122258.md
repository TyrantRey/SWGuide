**☞ November Second Update**

**▶IMPROVEMENT – Beginner / Returnee Function**
- New Attendance Check will be added for Beginners and Returnees.

- For Beginner / Returnee: Dedicated Mark (Beginner / Returnee) is displayed instead of Rank Mark.
> Beginner / Returnee Mark will be displayed during Team and Force stats.

- Beginner / Returnee can obtain additional rewards (Maze dropped Materials) in certain Mazes (Solo / Team stats).
- Team with Beginner / Returnee: Additional Rewards will be provided to ALL TEAM MEMBERS.
> To obtain Additional Rewards: Beginner / Returnee / Rest of Team Members must have 'Valid Entry'.
> If additional rewards can be obtained → it is displayed as an icon.

**▶ NEW RAID - Parallel Metronome**
- New Raid ‘Parallel Metronome’ will be updated.
>  Difficulty: Can play Solo
>  Difficulty: Can play as Solo and Team (1 ~ 4 Players)

- New Equipment Item can ONLY be obtained in Parallel Metronome.
