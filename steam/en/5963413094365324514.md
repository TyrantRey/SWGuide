# [Notice] Jin's Birthday Milestone Event

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/fd0d0d73c6ff0fb2f584bc0f519dad1bcb7c00fa.png)

Hello! This is an announcement from SoulWorker.

We would like to apologize for the delay in celebrating the birthday of our best fighter, Jin Seipatsu. During his recent mission, Jin was cornered by Lugman, but he managed to escape. Unfortunately, he sustained many wounds, so we decided to let him recover. But now he is back! Let's celebrate his birthday together!

Earn fantastic rewards by completing quests and clearing mazes! This is yet another milestone event for everyone to participate in! Don't miss out—login now, finish those quests, and run those mazes! See you in-game!

July 30th 16:00 ~ August 6th 15:59 GMT
