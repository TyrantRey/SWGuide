** Synopsis**
Rapid Flame follow the one and only rule of kneeling down to the stronger.

Flemma has knelt down before a god much stronger than he without any reason to assume otherwise. Wanting limitless power with limitless respect to those with more can commonly be spotted in Rapid Flame.

By that logic, Flemma willingly becomes a faithful servant to he who has great power and sets off to extinguish helpless insects that stand in their way.

He was not ashamed, nor was he sad. He was not in agony, nor was he full of regret.
Instead, he was happy staring down at the so-called heroes of humanity on this gigantic battlefield the powerful one had prepared for him.

Oh, you useless god, thank you for giving me the chance to burn all these humans all at once.

This Leader of Vessi can finally indulge in the ecstasy that could not formerly be found. This place is where you and your men will see your last battle, a battlefield filled sacrificed by SoulWorkers and humans alike.

This battlefield will be stained with the blood of humans in order to fill the savage king’s desires and his minions’ ecstasy of battle.

**☞ September 2nd Update **
**▶ Playable Character Balance Adjustment **
- All characters
> Skills concerning the Critical will be modified from Party-based to Personal.
> The Critical resistance of Raid Boss of Violent Sun will be decreased regarding this adjustment.
> Buffs regarding Attack Speed and Agility will be downgraded.
> The SV gain per combos will be adjusted, and new function regarding SV Recovery will be added to Advancement Passive.
> Other minimized modifications will be scheduled.

- Haru Estia
> More Skill damage inflicted, and long-stand skill motions will be shortened.
> Some skill mechanisms will be modified.
> Adjustment: First Blade, Power Slash, Leap Attack, Flash Step, Swords Fall, Trollsverd, Blade Quake, Slashing Warfare, Cross Strike, Endless Combat, Endless Fight

- Erwin Arclight
> More Skill damage inflicted, and some skill mechanisms will be modified.
> The skill  will be removed and modified to attack skill .
> Adjustment: Sky High, Rapid Fire, Gun Combination, Focus Shot, Combat Master, Guided Shot. Unexpected Fortune, Sprinter (Modified), Hide and Seek, Napalm Bomb, Blaze Hover, Swallow Bullet
