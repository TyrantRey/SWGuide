# [Notice] Price issue regarding the costume package [Followers of the Vacuum]

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/92dc2400b43404a93ece1df73fe2c3b6ced82bb3.png)

Hello, this is an announcement from SoulWorker.
We have fully acknowledged that Jin and Erwin's  Costume packages have been mispriced. Users who have purchased the costume mentioned above will get refunded for extra 350 SoulCash.

The detail is as follows:

-  Jin's Follower of the Vacuum
-  Erwin's Follower of the Vacuum

- 1,600 SoulCash (Previous) -> 1,250 SoulCash (After)

- 350 SoulCash will be compensated for each purchases mentioned above (After March 17th)

※ Compensation will be sequentially distributed.

SoulWorker will always try to provide a better quality of service.

Thank you.
