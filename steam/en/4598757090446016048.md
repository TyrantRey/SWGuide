# [Notice] Temporary Ranking System Shutdown

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/92dc2400b43404a93ece1df73fe2c3b6ced82bb3.png)

Hello, this is SoulWorker Operation Team.

Some of the Ranking systems will be changed and reorganized in August First Update.
To apply changes, Ranking System will be DISABLED for a certain period of time.

Please refer to the following for details.

========================

Period: August 14th (Sun), 16:00  ~ August 18th (Thu) Update

1. August 14th (Sun), 16:00  ~ August 18th (Thu) Update: Not Possible to enter the "Ranking Menu".
2. Previous Ranking Rewards (Ranking Period: August 7th (Sun) 16:00 ~ August 14th (Sun) 15:59 ) will be able to obtain from August 18th (Thu) / After Update to August 21st (Sun) 15:59 .
3. (IMPORTANT) Must receive rewards within August 18th (Thu) / After Update to August 21st (Sun) 15:59 . (Not possible to obtain Ranking Rewards after the period.)
4. New Ranking System will be applied after the August 18th (Thu) Update.
========================

Please refer to the information above,
and if there is any additional alteration, it will be noticed separately.

Thank you.
