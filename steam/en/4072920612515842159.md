# [Notice] Modification on SoulCash Top-up System

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/3adcd2dc241ff4bcc2c84ee33eb6396c0aa19121.png)

Hello, this is from SoulWorker.
We are here to inform our beloved users that new modification will be made on terms of charging currency from TWD to USD while expanding our service into global regions. For further details, please refer to the information below.

****
30 TWD = 120 SoulCash

****
1 USD = 112 SoulCash

※ When the value of products will be determined based on USD, our set value of 4 SoulCash per 1 TWD will be remained the same. However, we apologize that the actual charging amount may vary due to the differences between exchange rates of TWD and USD.

****
