# [Notice] Event Reward Pre-distribution Issue

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/92dc2400b43404a93ece1df73fe2c3b6ced82bb3.png)

Hello, this is an announcement from SoulWorker.
In process of setting New Year Rewards on December 29th, we have checked that the rewards have been pre-distributed to some players of TC server.
Accordingly, the same rewards will be provided to all players except for ones who have already received the reward mail.
We apologize for this inconvenience.

Details are as follows:

- Subject to Grant : Players who have not received New Year Reward mail

- Detailed Rewards: Anti-Limiter *5, Twilight Resource *30,  Lunar Fall *1 (per account only once)

- Distribution Date: January 13th, 2022

Once again, SoulWorker Team sincerely apologizes for the inconvenience of your gameplay.
Thank you.
