# [Notice] 2021 May Update Preview

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/3adcd2dc241ff4bcc2c84ee33eb6396c0aa19121.png)

**☞ 1st Update in May**

**▶Global Service Expansion & Language Setting in 'English'**
With your great support, a new server has been added for English-speaking users, enabling them to play the English version of SoulWorker. Players using Traditional Chinese are able to edit the language setting from Chinese to English.

**▶Worker Diary**
- A new function will be added for you to track your in-game progress statistically.
- Your in-game progress will be shown per Account/Character.

**☞ 2nd Update in May**

**▶Skill Balance Update: Jin Seipatsu**
-Enhance to use more ‘Upgrade Counter Attack’
-Inflict more damage with shortened skill motions.
-New Class Up Passive Skill Set

**▶New Additional Facial Features**
- Blush and Kitty Mouse New Facial Features will be added.
![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/8809cbf443129eca2613820f42b7a42457c0a7c6.png)

**▶Brooch Simulators**
- You’ll be able to experience/get a taste of a brooch before applying it to your sockets.

**▶Buff Icons**
- Buffs will appear to look like the same form as skill icon for easier recognition.
![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/06e9f2670007a6ca533589be166da0e2a4ee627e.png)

**▶Akashic Record Total Release**
- More than one Akashic Record will be able to be released simultaneously.
![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/e3148d0cb53dd46a40bb79954c01a018d31872d9.png)

Please look forward to our updates coming soon in May!

Please be notified the updates may be altered due to unprecedented internal issues.
