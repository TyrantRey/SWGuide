# [Notice] In-game error regarding League Raid Access

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/92dc2400b43404a93ece1df73fe2c3b6ced82bb3.png)

Hello, this is an announcement from SoulWorker.
We are here to inform you that the access Pop-Up regarding the Legion Invader (League Raid) has not been responded in  status. Internal modifications are currently in progress to fix this internal error that occurred after the First Update on November 11th, and please be notified that the error would be fixed after the Second Update on November 25th.
Details are as follows:

- No response was given from the League Raid access UI when being Force.

- It will be fixed after the Second Regular Maintenance (11/25)

SoulWorker Team sincerely apologize for this inconvenience and will try to provide a better environment for your smooth gameplay.

Thank you.
