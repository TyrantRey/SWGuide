# [Notice] 2021 October Update Preview

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/2dd01f5cba2cb62d0ded9d2005f929acf2db8373.png)

**◆ 2021 October Update Preview**

**☞ The First Update in October**

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/b518dc26b88997686f4f813c9a431f4cf0ca4b78.png)

**▶ Corrupted Record: Iris Yuma & Jin Seipatsu**

****
Iris Yuma, a woman who left people behind.
Jin Seipatsu, a man who stays to protect people.
A tale of betrayal and malice has been silently continued in ruined camp.
How powerful the energy of Desire is, leading people to such hell, and everything got messed up by what?
The whole truth will be revealed from the last record of Grasscover Camp.

**[3rd Corrupted Record: Iris and Jin’s Story] **
The Vacuum-based background stories of Iris Yuma and Jin Seipatsu will be newly released.
Records titled as Corrupted Bravery, Rage, Unity, and Passion can be purchased from NPC , and new episodes will be available when using it.
New quests regarding Corrupted Records will be accessible for characters above Lv. 68, and warp items will still be purchasable after completion of all quests mentioned above.

**▶ Fusion Process for Brooches (Possession) **
-  An adjustment regarding the fuse of unsellable Possession-stated brooches will be applied.
-  When fusing, five Possession-stated brooches are required and the consequence will be stated the same.
-  Fusion Process for Possession-stated brooches will require ‘Brooch Fusion’ as same as a standard one.

**▶ Possibility Increase on higher-grade Consequence of fusing brooches**
-  The possibility for getting higher-graded brooches will be increased when fusing standard brooches and that of possessed in comparison to the present.

**▶ Update on Twilight Equipment Series Upgrade Process **
     -     When upgrading, no longer destruction will be applied, and the success rate will be increased.
-	When refining, Twilight Resource will be required instead of Energy Converter.
-	When crafting, costs and Twilight Resource requirements will be adjusted.

**▶ Update on Leveling PKG **
-  Level 68 PKG including ‘Supportive Primacy Gear’ will be newly updated.
-  Disorders’ Gears from Level 65 PKG will be updated as ‘Supportive Disorders Gear’ that is distributed with random-fixed options.
-  Devourers’ Gear from Level 60 PKG will be removed.
-  Further includes will be partially modified.

**☞ The Second Update in October**

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/dc983d260fc9a9788d69fc4fbad1956cce606bc6.png)

**▶ New Maze ‘Scary Daydream’ **
-  A new daydream-themed maze ‘Scary Daydream’, which is much difficult than Second Daydream, will be newly added,
-  In comparison to the previous background of Daydream-themed mazes, you will face such scary monsters that Setsuka dreamed of at night.

**▶ Removal of The Primal Maze and update on Lunar Fall Reward**
- The Primal will no longer be serviced, and its access portal will be removed.
- Quests and requirements regarding The Primal will be modified.
- Achievements and Progression Rate regarding The Primal will still be continued in The Primal (Hero); previous values will be maintained.
- Main rewards from The Primal will be planned to be obtainable from Lunar Fall.

Please look forward to our Upcoming October Update.
Thank you.

※ The updates in detail may be altered due to unprecedented internal issues.
