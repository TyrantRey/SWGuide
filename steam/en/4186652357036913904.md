# [Notice] 2021 November Update Preview

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/6175be722a1c34cc5772f361753890c817e9e5c9.png)

**◆ 2021 November Update Preview**

**☞ November First Update**

**▶ LEAGUE SYSTEM IMPROVEMENTS**
- Attendance
 > Can obtain a League Attendance Reward when attending at the League window.
 > Can obtain Additional Rewards if more and more League members attend.
- League Shop
 > Can purchase items at the League Shop using League Funds.
 > Item purchases can be made regardless of League Position.
- League Window
 > The location of some icons will be changed, and functions will be improved. (e.g. Enable to see pictures of League Members)

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/1867d86d09dc1e4389b9655f1befd6abf59ebd18.png)

**▶ MAZE FOR LEAGUE - ‘LEGION INVADER’**
- Only the party with Same League Members is enabled to enter this maze.
- The League  can open the maze at any time of the week. Can obtain the rewards at the end of the Maze.
- Each member can enter only once when activated. The maze can be activated twice a week.
- When the maze is deactivated, rewards for monster defeat will be provided to all the participants who clear the maze.
- Enable to use Active/Passive Akashic Records.

**▶ ABYSS LOUNGE EXPANSION & RANKING MAZE CHANGE**
- Max Phase of Abyss Lounge, the current ranking maze, will be expanded.
- Ranking Maze Change: Second Daydream will be changed to Scary Daydream.
* For update, Ranking System will be off from 00:00 (GMT+8) on November 8th. It will be activated again after the update on November 11th.

**▶ SOUL PASS SEAON 1**
- New System: Can obtain Pass Points by Playing Game. Can receive Additional Rewards with Pass Points.
- Pass Rewards: Free Reward / Premium Reward (Purchase 'Pass Upgrade' To Unlock Premium Reward)
- Soul Pass will be open for a limited time.

**☞ November Second Update**

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/6c85efa2412c8f1bb4b36eb107a7c1496cee8ccb.png)

**▶ NEW MAZE - ‘LUNAR FALL HERO’**
- Lunar Fall Hero, a Superior version of Lunar Fall will be newly added.
- It requires a different attack strategy than the existing Luna Fall maze.
- Above Lv. 72 can enter. (Required Equipment Specifications: Same or Higher for 'Violent Sun' Phase 3 Clear)
- Lunar Fall Hero will be open for a limited time only.

**▶ ACCESSORY TAG**
- Tags for Accessories will be newly added.
- Tag Chip for Accessory Tag can be obtained through in-game play and cash shop.

**▶ CHARACTER BALANCE PATCH: LEE NABI**
- Some skills will be changed to make combat tactics easier than before.
> Skills for Balance Patch: SoulStrike Mastery / Reload / DestroNail / Bolt Enhancement / Save Energy / Triple Sniping / Hit And Run / Bust Driver / Honey Bee

Please look forward to our Upcoming November Update.
Thank you so much.

※ The update in detail may be altered due to unprecedented internal issues.
