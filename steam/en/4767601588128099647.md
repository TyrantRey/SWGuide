# [Notice] Client Patch (05/18)

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/3adcd2dc241ff4bcc2c84ee33eb6396c0aa19121.png)

Hello, this is the SoulWorker Operation Team.

- Our server will undergo a client patch in order to fix critical issues.
- The patch will be conducted without any maintenance and will be automatically downloaded upon quitting and restarting the game.
-The details are as follows:

** **
The graphic issues regarding alphabet-based language fonts except English has been fixed: (Subjects: French, German, Russian, Spanish, Ukrainian, Vietnamese, etc.)

1.	Character names appearing in black lettering.
2.	The disappearance of the Mini Map.
3.	Appearance of Unidentified Textures in intermittent condition.

We apologize for the inconvenience and will try to make a better gameplay environment for you all.
Thank you
