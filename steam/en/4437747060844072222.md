# [Notice] 2022 June Update Preview

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/6175be722a1c34cc5772f361753890c817e9e5c9.png)

**◆ 2022 June Update Preview**

**☞ June First Update**

**▶ ROCCO TOWN – **
![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/34cb8c4d161f346a853302eff48724ffb51071ee.png)
- Rocco Town Hero will be added.
* Control Zone 43 / R Square / Tower of Greed / Best Showtime
- Clear the  maze →  will be provided as a reward in proportion to the number of selected challenge options.
- ' Familiar Random Box' will be added in the  Crafting Tab (Gruton Coin Shop).

**▶ BROKEN SAVIOR – NEW COSTUME DROP**
![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/4973609e489e002e9da31e98def82c582dad6de6.jpg)
- Broken Savior New Drop Costume:
> Clear Broken Savior NORMAL (Phase 2) or Clear Broken Savior HARD
> Chance to obtain New Costume 'Distorted Justice'. (Very Low Drop Chance)
> This new costume is designed after Tenebris' outfit.

- 'Distorted Justice' Costume PKG: Can be Traded.

**▶ SURPLUS SOUL WEAPON HOLOGRAM COSTUME**
- New Weapon Costume Mystery Box will be added to Gruton Coin Shop.
- This new mystery box contains Soul Weapon Hologram modeled after a Low-Level Weapon appearance.

**▶ GAME SYSTEM CHANGE – PHASE 1**
- Some items (materials, etc) from Hidden Hideout / Lunar Fall can be sold at the shop. (Enable to gain a certain amount of Zenny.)
- Cost of Brooch Extract will be reduced.
- New NPC will be added.
> Some of Cash Items can be purchased with Zenny through this New NPC.
- Gruton Coin Shop:
> Some items' Purchase Limit will be removed.
> Market related Items will be deleted.
> Low-Grade Brooch related Items will be deleted.
- Raid Entry Limit will be changed.
* Details will be delivered through the Update Note.
* Game System Change (Sync with Korea Server) will be applied after continuous internal reviews.

**☞ June Second Update**

**▶ NEW MAZE – ‘THE BEYOND’**
![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/ddda4c1b1d56cce69582ef1ace4b3771a0cbf973.png)
- Above Lv. 76 can enter.
- Defeat all the Desire Workers and KENT who emerge in District 6 where Desire Energy overflows!
- There may be a chance to obtain [1st~3rd Grade] Accessory Re-Tag Chip.

**▶ NEW EVENT -  ‘VACATION GHOST STORY’**
![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/94161d4bbbc82577cfd287b8153e5a43261be952.png)
- After a series of events, SoulWorkers go to Summer Vacation! Yay! …But no one expects that strange events are waiting for them.
- This is a Scenario Event that experiences ghost stories of a vacation spot.

**☞  ABOUT JULY UPDATE**
