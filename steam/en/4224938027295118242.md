# [Notice] 211223 Update Note Preview

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/6175be722a1c34cc5772f361753890c817e9e5c9.png)

**Hello! This is an announcement from SoulWorker.
Here is the Update and Event News for December 23, 2021.**

**---------------------------------------------◆ UPDATE ◆---------------------------------------------**

**1. CHARACTER SKILL CHANGE: IRIS YUMA**
- 2 skills of Iris will be changed to new skills.
① Spiral Pierce → Fortress Cannon
 > The cannon barrel gathers energy and drags the enemy. Then, blow away the enemy with the Energy Sphere.
② Full Burst → Liberator
 > It explodes energy and fires it forward. The closer the distance, the more damage it causes to the enemy.

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/42d15cb4f6a9d99eacb3d83b67f3071531246d73.png)
* (Before Update) Skill Points that applied to Spiral Pierce / Full Burst → (After Update) Automatically apply to Fortress Cannon / Liberator

**4. MORE CHANGES AND FIXES**
