# [Notice] 2021 August Update Preview

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/7adcdb549abfe51a1ebfc5934a039625bb2fb588.png)

**◆ 2021 August Update Preview**

### **☞ August 1st Update**

**▶ Playable Character Skill Balance Update:**

- Lily Bloommerchen
> Skill damages have been increased and Cooldowns for her Advancement Passive will be modified. .
> Skills on Adjustment:
Evil Slash, Spinning Devil, Scythe Cutter, Grim Countdown, Death Scythe, Evil Trinity, Bloody Blossom, Leashed Beast, Death Grinder, and Driving Insanity

- Iris Yuma
> Damages will be increased, and some of her skill mechanism will be adjusted.
> Skills on Adjustment:
Pulse Fire, Camel Spin (Spike Blade), Full Burst (Slash), Earthquake (Volcano), Operation Ashes, Electric Cannon, Rapid Vulcan, and Bullet Storm

- Stella Unibell
 > Damages will be increased, and some of her skill mechanism will be adjusted.
 > The healing portion of HP and SG Recovery skills will be decreased.
 > Skills on Adjustment:
Trouble Pass, Beads Play, Beast Parade, Soul Scream, Sound Wave, Bounce Wave, Decade Pulse, Black Morass, Bass Attack, Wolves’ Showtime, Phantom Release, Hysteric Shock, Melody of Sorrow, Souldash Attack, SoulStrike Mastery, Wheel Dance, Best Tempo

**▶ Other Modifications**
- A sub-functional option of supporting fewer clicks when using such skills that required consecutive mouse clicks will be newly added.
- An option of showing the exact time when chatting will be newly added.
- A function of Warning Pop-ups when Party/Force requested from Blacklist will be newly added.
- A sub-functional option of widening the point of view in Raid / World Boss will be newly added.
