**▶ RAID – TRAGIC FAIRY **
- Tragic Fairy  will be updated.
-  Mode: Can challenge as Solo or Party (1 ~ 4 Players)

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/89a57640ec574eecb44a0a610de0c310a21c43d0.png)

**[ ENTRY REQUIREMENT ]**
- Above Lv. 79 can enter.

**[ VALID ENTRY ]**
* FP Consumption per Entry: FP 20
- Tragic Fairy  doesn't have any Entry Limit. (e.g. If a player has enough FP, No limit to enter the raid.)
- However, Tragic Fairy  has a "Valid Entry" limit.

- Valid Entry: The actual number that a player can obtain Drop Items if a player clears the raid.
> Fail to clear the Raid within Valid Entry Limit → No Reward and Lose 1 chance of Valid Entry Limit
> Leaving in the middle of the Raid → No Reward and Lose 1 chance of Valid Entry Limit

- The number of valid entries (for MANIAC) is 7 per week and will be added 2 times when Soul "PLUS" is activated.
- When a player enters the raid → the Valid Entry Limit will be immediately deducted.
- No Valid Entry Left means: No Drop or Reward Items can be obtained.
- Valid Entry Limit will be "Reset Mondays at 1 AM (GMT)".
- The number of valid entries (for MANIAC) will not be shared with valid entries (for NORMAL / HARD).
* Not possible to enter with Force status.

**[ MORE ABOUT TRAGIC FAIRY: MANIAC ]**
- Total Phase: 2
> Ferdelance  Mode / Phase 2 = Ferdelance  Mode / Phase 1
> Ferdelance  Mode / Phase 2 → Players will encounter the most powerful version of Ferdelance.

- Respawn Limit:
> Free Respawn - [3] +2 (PLUS)
> Respawn (with Items) - [3]
> Cash Respawn -
- Not possible to use ACTIVE Akashic Record

- Two(2) Repeat Quests will be added: Daily (1) and Weekly (1)
> Repeat Quest: Arcadia Realm → Speak to  →  Tab → Can purchase ‘Daily Operation Receiver’ and ‘Weekly Operation Receiver’. (Quest tab)
> Daily / Weekly Operation Receiver: Can purchase Once a day per Account / Repeat Quest - Can accept and complete Once a day

- New Achievements for Tragic Fairy  will be added.
- A New Crafting Material will be added.
