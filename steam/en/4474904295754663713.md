# [Notice] 2022 July Update Preview

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/6175be722a1c34cc5772f361753890c817e9e5c9.png)

**◆ 2022 July Update Preview**

**☞ July First Update**

**▶ SOLO RAID**
- Solo Raid
> Violent Sun  / Broken Savior  will be updated
> Enable to enter through NPC  in Grace City.
> Overall style of raid strategies will remain the same. (Time Limit / Phase / and so on)
> Some patterns of Boss and HP will be slightly changed.

**▶ CHANGE - **
- Merchants' Union VIP Membership Card
> Not Possible to purchase at Cash Shop.
> Can be purchased at Yomi’s Shop with Zenny.

**▶ CHANGE – **
- NPC Shops
> Sales Tabs will be improved and some of Sales Items will be changed.

- Yomi's Shop
> Some of items will be added.
> Items that will be removed:
→ Dual Mist
→ Bit / Kilo / Mega Intelligence Pill
→ Bit / Kilo / Mega Runners Pill
→ Bit / Mega / Giga Soul Mist
> 'Magic Hammer' item: Yomi's Shop → Zyte's Shop

- Bucker TV's Shop
> Some of items will be added.
> Items that will be removed:
→ Bit / Quarter Stamina Ade
→ Bit / Kilo / Mega / Semi Strength Pill
→ Bit / Kilo / Mega / Semi Vital Pill
→ Bit / Kilo / Mega Intelligence Pill
→ Bit / Kilo / Mega Runners Pill
→ Bit / Kilo / Mega Agility Pill
→  Steel Graves

**▶ CHANGE – Log-in Reward / Stay Connected Reward**
- Consecutive Attendance / Monthly Attendance / Stay Connected (30 mins / 60 mins)
> Rewards will be changed.

- Monthly Attendance Rewards
> Unified TERA Brooch Cube (Possession) and Parable's A.R Transmitter [★4~★5] will be added.

- Stay Connected (30 mins) and Stay Connected (60 mins) Rewards will be changed to be the same.

**▶ CHANGE – Some Parts of In-game Graphics**
- Districts / Characters
> Lighting Effects and Shader will be changed.

**☞ August Update**
- SoulWorker SEASON 2: Road to East will be updated with new stories!
- New System: Soul Mate
> Soul Mate: This is a pet system.
> Soul Mates have Auto-Looting ability.
> Some of them provide Useful Effects to Characters.

Please look forward to our Upcoming July Update.
Summer is finally upon us and that means it’s time to have some fun outside, playing games with friends.
Thank you so much!
