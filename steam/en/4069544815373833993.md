# [Notice] 2021 June Update Preview

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/3adcd2dc241ff4bcc2c84ee33eb6396c0aa19121.png)

### ◆ June 2021 Updates Preview

**☞ 1st June Update**

**▶ Skill Balance Adjustments: Chii Aruel**
-  Damage on certain Skills that have been lower than others will be enhanced.
Cool Down regarding Black Blood Skills will be reduced.
-  Charging-related Skills will be available for quicker use.
-  Motion Delays after the use of some Skills will be shortened.
-  Amount of HP recovery from SoulStrike Mastery will be slightly lowered.

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/baa1c23a01ccf1836ad677278734517383ea8a4a.jpg)

**▶ Event Maze ‘Soul Jumper’**
-  is an event maze in which special jumping skills can be used to overcome obstacles in order to reach the final goal.
- Enable to challenge  during the Event Period.
- Enable to exchange many items with the event crafting materials obtained from the Event Maze during the Event Period.

**☞ 2nd June Update**

**▶ Skill Balance Adjustments: Ephnel**
- Reloading Functions on some Skills will be removed.
 The  Buff will be attainable more easily with a lower number of required reloads.
- Damage per Hit regarding SoulPlus Skills of Field Octagon will be reduced.
However, adjustments will be made so that more Attacks will be possible.
- Duration of Support Call applied to Personal / Party will be adjusted.
- Damage per Hit regarding some Skills will be enhanced.

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/dd25a4d0a5f00ee62a7569d34c37975bce0260ab.jpg)

- Enable to exchange many items with the event crafting materials obtained from the Event Maze during the Event Period.
