# [Notice] World Boss Closing

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/92dc2400b43404a93ece1df73fe2c3b6ced82bb3.png)

Hello, this is an announcement from SoulWorker.

We are fully acknowledged that our users continuously expect the World Boss in Tenebris (EN) server that has been opened since May 13th.
The World Boss, as a time-limited content, is required much participation to defeat a huge scale of Boss, providing incomparable experience and following high quality of rewards.
With all due respect, there are many difficulties of providing the contents in our current servers in regard to its specialty as the multiplayer-fighting content, and simultaneous accessibility from each far area/country.
Even though we have done our best to provide the World Boss to users in Tenebris (EN) server, we concluded that it will not be able to service the contents mentioned above in a smooth environment.

As a consequence, system change will be applied to the World Boss-based rewards, enabling them to be obtainable in a different way.
SoulWorker Team sincerely apologizes for such inconvenience.
Details are as follows:

**1)  Stay Connected Reward:**
- Rewards for being played for 90 minutes will be replaced with the new boosting item.
The boosting will be the same benefit as 50 times defeat of The Doom and removed after 48 hours after receiving the item. Also, it can be duplicated with another boosting.
- Benefit:
 +  +
+  (Duration: 60 minutes)
- Regarding this change, the previous rewards for both 30 and 60 minutes will be partially modified and the total of Stay Connected rewards will also be better supplied compared to the past.

**2) Daily Mission **
- Previous World Boss-based drops can be received from the completion of Daily Mission that will be newly applied.
Reward:  +  +  +  +
- The requirement for the daily mission will be the completion of ‘Violent Sun 3 times’ and it will be continuously changed to the hardest following raid.
- With the system change mentioned above, weekly crafting restriction for Brooch from NPC  will be newly added.

All information in detail will be applied to both Rosca (TC) and Tenebris (EN) servers and planned to be conducted in the middle of October.
When being applied, the playable World Boss in Rosca (TC) will be coming to an end.
Throughout this modification applied to our system from limited contents to general gameplay, any characters bearing with Violent Sun will also get all rewards from World Boss.
Regarding this change, our goal is to provide a more comfortable and faster-reacted environment to our users. Further details will be announced on our official upcoming update note.
SoulWorker Team sincerely apologizes again for the inconvenience concerning World Boss playing, and we are looking forward that this change will be more helpful for all users’ smooth gameplay.

Thank you.
