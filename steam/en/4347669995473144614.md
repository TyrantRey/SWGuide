# [Notice] April Fool’s Day Boosting Issue (EN)

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/92dc2400b43404a93ece1df73fe2c3b6ced82bb3.png)

Hello, this is an announcement from SoulWorker.
We have checked that April Fool’s Day Boosting was not applied on EN server.
Accordingly, the same boosting will be re-provided during a specific period.
We apologize for this inconvenience.

Details are as follows:

- The boosting that was supposed to provide from April 1st to April 4th during the April Fool’s Day Event period not being applied on EN server
- The same boosting will be re-applied from April 8th to April 11th on EN server.

Once again, SoulWorker Team sincerely apologizes for the inconvenience of your gameplay.
Thank you.
