# [Notice] In-Game Known Issues

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/92dc2400b43404a93ece1df73fe2c3b6ced82bb3.png)
Hello, this is an announcement from SoulWorker.

We have been checked that our users worldwide are experiencing an error in the game.
We apologize for this inconvenience.

Details are as follows:

 - The error regarding no Refinement Selection Points accumulations when using  Energy Converter
- The error regarding being unable to restart Lonely Christmas Maze

- Refinement Selection Points as many as  Energy Converter crafted from Event I of Gruton Coin Shop and used by January 13th maintenance will be applied after the same maintenance. (per character)
- If owning the max Refinement Selection Points(500) when January 13th maintenance, the points will be added to over 500. (No loss of previous Refinement Selection Points)

Once again, SoulWorker Team sincerely apologizes for the inconvenience regarding an error and your gameplay.

Thank you.
