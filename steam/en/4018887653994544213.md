# [Notice] Early-Closure Compensation for Collaboration Event

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/7adcdb549abfe51a1ebfc5934a039625bb2fb588.png)

Hello, this is an announcement from SoulWorker.

We are here to inform you that the 'KonoSuba - God's Blessing On This Wonderful World' event has been closed earlier than prior-notice, due to miscomprehension from Lion Games without authorization from IP holder.
SoulWorker Team has been prepared some compensation as an apology. Please check your H.E.M.S, and we will try to concern upcoming events in detail.

- Item Distribution for the early closure of KonoSuba Event via H.E.M.S

1) Compensation for early-closure
> TERA Brooch Select Option (Possession) x1, Energy Converter x10

2) Compensation for yet-crafted items
> All yet-crafted items listed except for KonoSuba Photos [1 day]
*KonoSuba Masques will be included in the listing mentioned above.

- Duration for Compensation
> Before the October 7th regular maintenance

We sincerely apologize for such inconvenience and try to ensure a better quality of service.
Thank you.
