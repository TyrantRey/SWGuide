# [Notice] In-game issues regarding a specific character (Lee Nabi) [Edited]

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/7adcdb549abfe51a1ebfc5934a039625bb2fb588.png)

Hello, this is an announcement from SoulWorker.

We have fully acknowledged that users may feel uncomfortable with the issue concerning the durability of Lee Nabi’s equipment. Modifications are currently carried out to fix this issue, and please be notified that it will be fixed after the 1st update in August.

Details are as follows:

****
- An error is that durability of Lee Nabi's equipment has not decreased
- An error is that Lee Nabi characters are unable to clear the Daily Mission for Repair

** **
- The issue will be fixed after the 1st update in August
- Duration of the two items mentioned below will be extended to August 19th:
└  Anti-Destruction Device /  Energy Converter
- The Supply Box event will be finished on August 5th, since we have announced it previously.
   However, the crafting period for event material will last for two weeks longer after finished.
   (~ Update on August 19th)

** **
- Eligibility: Lee Nabi character above Level 45
- Rewards: Gruton Coins x1400,  Special Mission Unit Supply Boxes x840
- Date: All rewards will be distributed to the  tab of characters’ H.E.M.S after the 1st maintenance in August.

We sincerely apologize for the inconvenience and will try to provide a better quality of service we can.
Thank you.
