- New Function: Reset All League Skills
 > League skills will be able to reset by using the League Fund.
 > Can be used by the League Master only. Require 3,000,000 League Fund to use Reset All League Skills.

****
- Attendance Rewards: Individual attendance + League Members' attendance on each day of the week

****
- How To Use: Find NPC  - 'Shop' function

**2. MAZE FOR LEAGUE - ‘LEGION INVADER’**

****
- Above Lv. 55 can enter.
- Only the Same League Members are enabled to enter this maze.

****
- The League  can open the maze at any time of the week.
- It requires 10,000 Zenny (League Fund) to activate the maze.
- The maze can be activated twice a week. It lasts 48 hours after the maze is activated.
- 1 ~ 4 player parties can enter.
- Each member can enter only once when activated.
- For 2 ~ 4 player parties, only the Team Leader can apply for entry to the maze.
- Activation Weekly Reset: Mondays at 01:00 (GMT)
- Unable to activate 30 minutes before the activation weekly reset time and the activated maze will also be closed.

****
- The maze runs for a total of 5 minutes only, and it automatically ends after 5 minutes.
- There is a total of 15 phases of monster defeat.
- Enable to gain a score assigned to monsters when defeating monsters.
- No scores on monsters that are automatically killed when the maze ends.
- 'My Score' can be found in league records and the league raid activation button.
- The total score obtained by the league members will be initialized on a weekly basis.
- Enable to use Active/Passive Akashic Records.
- No limit to use Respawn.
- No Item / Zenny / Ether drops. Also, no EXP / SV gains.
- A member who has participated the league raid cannot be expelled or leave from the League until the maze is deactivated.

****
- League Raid Coin is the reward. Enable to purchase items with this coin from NPC .
- The League Raid Reward will be given according to individual clear score. It will be sent to the player's H.E.M.S of  tab.
