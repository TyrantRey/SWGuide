# [Notice] Soulworker Official China Service Expansion

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/d0f343240d222afcf346c478d79025ad8d836f7d.jpg)

Hello! This is an announcement from Soulworker.

We are thrilled to announce that Soulworker is expanding its services to China!

Starting Thursday, August 22, 2024, following scheduled maintenance, we will launch a new server named CN. This new server aims to provide a better and improved environment for our Chinese players and will feature exciting events running until September 5, 2024. Additionally, the game will now offer Simplified Chinese localization to support the Kent Server launch.

We deeply appreciate the continued support of all our Soulworker players. Our commitment remains to provide you with the best gaming experience possible, and we look forward to making your time in Soulworker even more enjoyable.

Thank you for being a valued member of our community!
