# [Notice] Merchant Union Bank VIP Card Re-distribution

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/92dc2400b43404a93ece1df73fe2c3b6ced82bb3.png)

Hello, this is SoulWorker Operation Team.

After the March 3rd maintenance, ‘Merchant Union Bank VIP Cards’ used previously have been distributed to H.E.M.S. by initializing Bank Expansion Data.

If the Bank expansion has been reached to the maximum count, please contact to our customer center: https://liongames.zendesk.com/hc/en-us

After the reclamation, the rest will be exchanged to ‘Merchants' Union VIP Membership Card’.

If your personal Inventory also being expanded up to max,
‘Merchant Union Bank VIP Cards’ will be exchanged to “Energy Converter” per card.

Thank you.
