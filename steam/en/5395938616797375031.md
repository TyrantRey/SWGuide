# [Notice] Trade Restriction System Change - Cash Shop Product

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/757a1b6a05608f23b618095734082db46191f0e0.png)

Hello, this is SoulWorker Operation Team.
Changes in the Trading Restriction Mechanism for Cash Items purchased in the Cash Shop will be applied sequentially through the first (Dec 7th) and second (Dec 21st) updates in December, 2023.
The details are as follows:

※ Users with More than 51 Cumulative Attendance days (ACCOUNT BASIS):
> Purchase Costumes and Costume PKG →

※ Users with Less than 51 Cumulative Attendance days (ACCOUNT BASIS):
> Purchase Costumes and Costume PKG →

※ Trading **(Market/Personal Transaction/H.E.M.S)** is restricted for 30 days after the first purchase of Cash Items in the Cash Shop. (ACCOUNT BASIS)
> Accounts with a record of purchasing Cash Items in Cash Shop  can use the trading system normally.
> For accounts with NO record of purchasing Cash Items in Cash Shop , the trading system will be restricted for 30 days upon the first purchase of Cash Items in Cash Shop.

※ The minimum level required to use the Trading Function is raised to Level 82. (ACCOUNT BASIS) **(Already Applied on December 7th Update)**

※ The Trading Restriction Mechanism applied after February 23rd Update will be DELETED.
