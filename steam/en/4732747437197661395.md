- 'Ascending Tornado' Skill:
> It will be affected by Attack Speed.
> Damage will be changed due to the modification of a skill structure.

**6. Lee Nabi**

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/57d53c1369ced84b9a0e29fe6e70e77a8bc42d1d.png)

- 'Bouncing Betty' Skill:
> If Additional Command is made while using the Bouncing Betty → Enable to use <Immediate Landing> or <Other Skill Combos>

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/a5d5f22a9002f7b189581b4f80bd104e692de087.png)

- 'Bust Driver' / 'Honey Bee' / 'Chainsaw' / 'DestroNail' Skills:
> SG Consumption will be Reduced.

*※  In the Test environment, we have changed Cooldown Time (Reduction) of Lee Nabi's skills. → As a result, Nabi's gameplay difficulty became too easy.
> So, we have decided not to reduce Cooldown Time, but to improve the 'Save Energy' Buff.*
