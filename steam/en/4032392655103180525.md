# [Notice] Modification for mispriced Costume Package - Ephnel

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/7adcdb549abfe51a1ebfc5934a039625bb2fb588.png)

Hello, this is an announcement from SoulWorker.
We have fully acknowledged that Ephnel’s  Costume packages have been mispriced.

Users who have purchased the costume mentioned above with 2,000 SoulCash will be consecutively compensated for extra 100 SoulCash. The price of the package is currently modified from 2,000 to 1,900 SoulCash.
We apologize for this inconvenience.

Further details are as follows:

2,000 SoulCash

1,900 SoulCash

- TC (Rosca): The purchases made from November 12th (Ephnel Update) to the modification held on July 5th.
- EN (Tenebris): The purchases made from May 13th (Global Service) to the modification held on July 5th.
※ Compensation will be sequentially distributed.
We always appreciate with your love and support for SoulWorker and will always try to provide a better quality of service.

Thank you.
