# [Notice] In- game error regarding Stella Unibell’s Skill

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/7adcdb549abfe51a1ebfc5934a039625bb2fb588.png)

Hello, this is an announcement from SoulWorker.

We are here to inform you that a part of Stella’s skill balances have been misadjusted. Modifications are currently carried out to fix such issues that occurred after the 1st maintenances in August, and please be notified that these would be fixed after the 2nd Update in August.
Details are as follows:

- Skill: Beads Play
: Staccato (SoulPlus) attack counts has been excessively set
- Skill: Decayed Pulse
: The Received Damage buff has been applied when using the skill.

We sincerely apologize for this inconvenience, and SoulWorker will try to provide a better environment for your smooth gameplay.

Thank you.
