# [Notice] In-Game Known Issues

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/92dc2400b43404a93ece1df73fe2c3b6ced82bb3.png)

Hello, this is an announcement from SoulWorker.
We have been checked that our users worldwide are experiencing an error in the game.
An internal investigation is currently in progress to fix this error, and further details will be announced when being fixed.
We apologize for this inconvenience.

Details are as follows:

 - Fix on the error regarding no Refinement Selection Points Restoration of  Energy Converter used from January 7th to January 13th maintenance

- Refinement selection points, unobtainable from the uses of  Energy Converter during the Lonely Christmas event period mentioned above, will be applied after January 27th maintenance. (per character)
- If the points have been accumulated as maximum (500) before 27th maintenance, it will be added without loss of previous points.

Once again, SoulWorker Team sincerely apologizes for the inconvenience regarding an error and your gameplay.

Thank you.
