# [Maintenance] Postpone Maintenance on April 25th

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/7c7f6ebdf340a66fdecc0dd9cb9d39e71b377ace.png)

Hello, this is an announcement from SoulWorker.

We regret to inform you that the regular server maintenance scheduled for April 25, 2024, has been postponed due to technical difficulties. We understand the inconvenience this may cause, and we sincerely apologize for any disruption to your gaming experience.

The maintenance will now take place on May 2, 2024, instead. We appreciate your patience and understanding as we work to ensure the smooth functioning of our servers.

In light of this postponement, please note that the following events will continue until the rescheduled maintenance date:

**Blooming Wildflower**: Note that this item is set to expire on April 25. Once received, it will automatically be deleted.
**Hidden Hideout / Rocco Town - Hero**: Please be aware that you will not be able to craft any more Energy Converters during this period.
**Akashic Record Dismantling**: There won't be any issues if you continue to dismantle your Akashic Records.

For further details about these events, please refer to our previous posts or visit the link provided below.

Link: https://vfun-lounge.valofe.com/page/soulworker/Update/15303

Once again, we apologize for any inconvenience caused by this change. Thank you for your understanding and continued support.
