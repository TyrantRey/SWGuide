# [Notice] 2022 May Update Preview

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/6175be722a1c34cc5772f361753890c817e9e5c9.png)

**◆ 2022 May Update Preview**

**☞ May First Update**

**▶ MAZE SYSTEM CHANGE - **
- Appearance patterns of monsters will change.
- The scoring system will change.
- " Abyss Lounge" Quest: The quest reward system will change.
> (Now) Clear Abyss Lounge → Reward: BP
> (After Update) Clear Abyss Lounge → Reward: BP (Quantity will be reduced) and Reward Item
> NPC : Can produce an item with a crafting material (Reward Item).

**▶ NEW SOLO RANKING MAZE - **
- This solo ranking maze contains Phase 1 of Flemma in 'Violent Sun'.
- Only Phase 1 is available. There will be no additional Phase.
- (After Entry) SV - Not recover at all / Skills - Not possible to use
- (Approaching to nearly around Flemma) SV - Recover 100% / Skills - Possible to use / Solo Ranking Timer - Activated

**▶ SOME CHANGES OF SOLO RANKING MAZE - **
- HP of RaphaKumba will be adjusted higher than it is now.
- (After Entry) SV - Not recover at all / Skills - Not possible to use
- (Approaching to nearly around RaphaKumba) SV - Recover 100% / Skills - Possible to use / Solo Ranking Timer - Activated

**▶ CHANGE AND NOTICE ABOUT RANKING MAZE**
- 'Scary Daydream' (Solo Maze) will be removed from Ranking Maze Lists.
- 'Flemma' (Solo) will be added to Ranking Maze Lists. And Reward Score by Maze will also be changed.
- To apply these changes, Ranking System will be DISABLED. (Period: May 9th (Mon) ~ May 12th (Thu) Update)
- Previous Ranking Rewards (Ranking Period: May 2nd (Mon) ~ May 8th (Sun)) will be able to obtain from the Ranking Menu within a certain period (May 12th (Thu) ~ May 15th (Sun)).

**▶ RAID BOSS DROP WEAPON COSTUME**
- Raid Weapon Costumes will be added to Each Raid after the update.
- Raid Weapon Costume: Weapon Costumes with the Appearance of Raid Boss Drop Weapons
- Very Low Drop Rate

**▶ SPECIFICATION CHANGE - 'PALE ASHES' EQUIPMENT**
- Pale Ashes equipment will not be destroyed by Upgrade.
- Increase the probability of Upgrade Success
- Reduce material quantity used for Upgrade.
- Option Refinement of Pale Ashes equipment: Change to use 'Burning Embers'.
- Can obtain the Pale Ashes equipment slightly less difficult than ever. (Reduced Crafting Materials / Drop Rate Up)

**▶ IMPROVEMENT - LEVEL UP REWARD PKG**
- Level 60 Reward PKG
> Add: Familiar Support Box Lv.1 / Supportive Disorders' I +9 Gear Set
> Remove: Supportive Void +9 Box

- Level 65 Reward PKG
> Add: Familiar Support Box Lv.2 / Supportive Primacy +9 Gear Set
> Remove: Supportive Disorders' I +9 Gear Set

- Level 68 Reward PKG
> Add: Familiar Support Box Lv.3 / Supportive Twilight +9 Gear Set
> Remove: Supportive Primacy +9 Gear Set

**☞ May Second Update**

**▶ BROKEN SAVIOR –  MODE**
-  Mode of Broken Savior in which you can see Tenebris immersed in powerful Desire Energy will be added.
- Entrances to  Mode will not be shared with Entrances to  Mode.
- Can obtain Picaresque Hero Weapons and Lv. 76 Familiars.
- Modification of Lv. 76 Familiars (After Update): Can be crafted with Crafting Materials obtained in Broken Savior  Mode.

**▶ ADDITION OF 'SCENARIOS'**
- New Main Quests will be added.
- Season 1 Epilogues that take place after the Tenebris incident will be available for your enjoyment.

**※ UPDATES AFTER JUNE 2022**
- Some specifications of current in-game mechanisms will be modified to match that of Korean server.
- Various inconveniences found throughout gameplay such as No. of Entrances, item trading and prices will be updated in an orderly fashion.
- Finally, many aspects of the game will be modified so that all regions will be able to enjoy the game with approximately the same mechanisms along with the beginning of 'Season 2'.
- Specification modification will be announced as soon as they are confirmed.

Please look forward to our Upcoming May Update.
Hope your month is positively a-MAY-zing!
Thank you so much!

※ The update in detail may be altered due to unprecedented internal issues.
