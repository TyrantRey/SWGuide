# [Notice] Restriction on Unusual Trade

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/58d91bfae0aebe3c4bfde577353b240ad12d7618.png)

Hello, this is SoulWorker Operation Team.

Investigations have been conducted for such violations concerning illegal program use and unusual trade of in-game currency. Although no information in detail has been announced, restrictions have been imposed on accounts in relation to such actions mentioned above.
The reason for investigating the Market trade record and that of Zenny is to prevent Real Money Trading (RMT) and stabilize the Market system.

Details are as follows:
1. Overpriced item that has been sold above the average worth (higher than 30%) of the same item.
2. Trades committed with a very large amount of Zenny without any items.
3. Trading with illegal Program Users
4. Trade regarding a large amount of Zenny with unidentified resources, and exceptional trading records/etc.
5. Restrictions will also be given to previous trade when being confirmed as a violation mentioned above.

When being confirmed, Permanent Suspension will be given to those regarding such action followed by Operational Policy. No further measures will be applied to the consequence.

We ask all users to stop being interacted with Real Money Trading and unusual Zenny Trade.
SoulWorker Team will always try to ensure a Fair Environment for your gameplay.

Thank you.
