# [Notice] In-game Known Issues

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/55ee63a25754ab97c8cf32449b1c9b0f94cb060c.png)

Hello, this is an announcement from SoulWorker.
We have internally found an in-game issue related to Crafting Lists  of Gruton Coin Shop after August (Season 2) Update.

Further details are as follows:

****

-  Crafting Lists (Gruton Coin Shop): '  Ephnel's School Swimsuit' was registered instead of 'Ultimate Refined Stone (Possession)'.

****

- Remove '  Ephnel's School Swimsuit' from Gruton Coin Shop and Register 'Ultimate Refined Stone (Possession)' again. **(Currently Fixed)**

****

1. Users who ***Accessed the Game** (August 18 Update ~ Before September 1 Update):
> '** School Swimsuit Costume PKG**' will be provided.

2-1. Users who already Crafted '  Ephnel's School Swimsuit' from  of Gruton Coin Shop (August 18 Update ~ Before September 1 Update):
> ' School Swimsuit Costume PKG' will **NOT** be provided.

2-2. Users who already Crafted '  Ephnel's School Swimsuit' from  of Gruton Coin Shop (August 18 Update ~ Before September 1 Update):
> '** Gruton Medal**' will be provided as much as you spent to craft '  Ephnel's School Swimsuit'.

*** Game Access Record: Users who accessed the game → Select Character window → Accessed to Districts**

*** Compensation H.E.M.S will expire on October 1st, 2022.**

We sincerely apologize for this inconvenience,
and SoulWorker will try to provide a better service for our users.

Thank you.
