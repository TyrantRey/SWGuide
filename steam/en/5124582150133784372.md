**1-1.  MUTED HAVEN**
![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/0b5b1bdd5256be34c737665a342df8c6b7f3a7aa.png)

▶ Recommended: Lv. 79 or Higher

***Highly advanced science is nothing short of magic...
and the precarious earth will eventually die and disappear.
You mortal beings are useless, and time is worthless.
Every life and hope will turn to dust and ashes...
Now Perish.***

Heitz and Martin have finally found the location where Vista is believed to be located. No time to hesitate. They immediately called SoulWorkers.

Once known as Paradise Island, Haven Island was rich in trade with Arcadia Realm. But now there is no light and no human footprint. And Heitz told SoulWorkers it's time to begin a manhunt for Vista... right here.

SoulWorkers have finally arrived in Haven Island. A... paradise? The island is completely in ruins.
All of a sudden, the Vacuum Junk appears and attacks SoulWorkers. Something... Something's not right... They realize that bodies are Heavier than usual.

Heitz constantly coughs on her way to the lab. Eventually, she can't stand the pain and falls down.
SoulWorkers decide to leave Haven Island immediately and return to Arcadia Realm... to save her.
After arriving, they visit Sedurin to ask why Heitz is sick.

Sedurin has asked SoulWorkers to go back to Haven Island with her Signal Detector.
They hope this device finds out a certain signal that made Heitz sick and fall.

**1-2.  CHAMBER ASTRAY**

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/7602f27c5d6507fe58d8e24c36a8636e2eefda7c.png)

▶ Recommended: Lv. 80 or Higher

***Ah yes. Science... It shall fulfill my dream, my ambition... my everything.
Yes, yes! Eat every single life and take all the souls in this pathetic world!
YOU... Make Me Complete!***

Heitz was restored to health without any further problems. And SoulWorkers killed all the enemies at the laboratory entrance.
Now's the time for them to enter the lab of Vista. Heitz also called Thomas to the front of the laboratory, and she told him to go into the lab with the SoulWorkers...

No one had a map of the lab. But this place was too big and wide. SoulWorkers and Thomas were afraid they'd miss Vista here...
They were at a loss for how to find their destination. Just then, a mysterious girl appeared in front of them. The girl told them where to go to meet Vista.
And... she suddenly disappeared in a flash.

**1-3.  STORY FOR EACH CHARACTER**

1. Haru Estia

Thomas tried to go to Haven Island to disrupt Vista's experiment. But Haru killed Vista's test subjects a step ahead of Thomas to stop and calm him down.

2. Erwin Arclight

Erwin found Rona looked very tired than usual. He asked Rona if she was okay. Then, she told him that it was because the Coffee Truck failed to arrive due to the attack of Vacuum Junk. Rona also said that C-ration Coffee cannot be defined as coffee. Now, Erwin headed to Haven Island... to find authentic regular coffee.

3. Lily Bloommerchen

A boy didn't give up and kept trying to go to the island to find his parents. Lily promised the boy that she would find his parents instead. Then she headed to Haven Island.

4. Jin Seipatsu

Jin heard there was a unit out of touch on the way back from operations on Haven Island. So, he entered the island to search for them.

5. Stella Unibell

Stella heard a rumor from Rona that a ghost was coming out of Haven Island. She said she would deal with everything related to the ghost and headed for the island.

6. Iris Yuma

Iris heard that Michelle, her mother, suddenly became unconscious with the flu. Sedurin said it could be a Monopuhl Leave Addiction, not the flu. And Rona told Iris about the conspiracy of Congress about Monopuhl Leaves. But the problem was that the existing drugs administered to Michelle... did not work at all. Iris headed to Haven Island to find relevant data.

7. Chii Aruel

Chii tried to find the source of the dog odor coming from somewhere. However, the smell came from various objects. She guessed that this smell may have come from Haven Island.

8. Ephnel

A soldier told Ephnel about his friend who went missing during an operation. He asked her to search the island. Then Ephnel told the soldier she would do the job for 1,000,000 Zenny, and he accepted it.

9. Lee Nabi

Nabi heard rumors from Heitz and Rona that ghosts appear on Haven Island. So, Nabi set off to the island to confirm the truth.

10. Dhana Opini

Dhana wanted to go to Haven Island and find traces of Vista. Heitz accepted her request.
