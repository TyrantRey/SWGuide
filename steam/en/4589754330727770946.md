**[ ENTRY REQUIREMENT ]**
- Above Lv. 79 can enter.

**[ HOW TO ENTER ]**
* Must Complete  Quests
→ Enable to enter Tragic Fairy through NPC  in Arcadia Realm

**[ VALID ENTRY ]**
* FP Consumption per Entry: FP 20
- Tragic Fairy doesn't have any Entry Limit. (e.g. If a player has enough FP, No limit to enter the raid.)
- However, Tragic Fairy has a "Valid Entry" limit.

- Valid Entry: The actual number that a player can obtain Drop Items if a player clears the raid.
> Fail to clear the Raid within Valid Entry Limit → No Reward and Lose 1 chance of Valid Entry Limit
> Leaving in the middle of the Raid → No Reward and Lose 1 chance of Valid Entry Limit

- The number of valid entries (for NORMAL / HARD) is 9 per week and will be added 2 times when Soul "PLUS" is activated.
- When a player enters the raid → the Valid Entry Limit will be immediately deducted.
- No Valid Entry Left means: No Drop or Reward Items can be obtained.
- Valid Entry Limit will be "Reset Mondays at 1 AM (GMT)".
* Not possible to enter with Force status.

**[ MORE ABOUT TRAGIC FAIRY ]**
- Total Phase: 2
- All Players Dead and No one have 'Respawn': Not possible to obtain rewards.

- Respawn Limit:
> Free Respawn - [3] +2 (PLUS)
> Respawn (with Items) - [3]
> Cash Respawn -

- 1 Repeat Quest will be added.
> Repeat Quest: Arcadia Realm → Speak to  →  Tab → Can purchase 'Disposable Mission Receiver' (45,000 Zenny)
> Disposable Mission Receiver: Can purchase Once a day per Account / Repeat Quest - Can accept and complete Once a day

- New Achievements for Tragic Fairy will be added.
- New Crafting Materials will be added.
> Flower Powder / Citrus Powder / Fused Crystal of Corruption
