# [Notice] Akashic Record Adjustment (August 26th Edited)

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/7adcdb549abfe51a1ebfc5934a039625bb2fb588.png)

Hello, this is an announcement from SoulWorker.

We are here to inform you that the New History's A.R Transmitter will be newly replaced as Parable's A.R Transmitter with the new arrival of Akashic Record 'Kent' on September 1st Update.

Details are as follows:

** **

* Rewards/Drops as New History's A.R Transmitter -> Replaced as Parable's A.R Transmitter.

**1. Drops from Boss(es) in Maze  **
New History's Novice A.R Transmitter -> Parable's Novice A.R Transmitter

**2. Drops from Primal (Hero) **
New History's Novice A.R Transmitter -> Parable's Novice A.R Transmitter

**3. Consecutive Attendance Reward (2nd day)  **
New History's A.R Transmitter -> Parable's A.R Transmitter

**4. Attendance Reward (11th/14th day) **
1) 11th: New History's High-grade A.R Transmitter -> Parable's High-grade A.R Transmitter
2) 14th: New History's High-grade A.R Transmitter -> Parable's High-grade A.R Transmitter

**5. Parable's A.R Transmitter can be crafted from Eo's list   **
Resource: Parable's Novice A.R Transmitter X 10
Limitation: 10 times (Weekly resets on every Monday, GMT 01:00)

**6. On Sales for Gruton CoinShop, Daily Mission Reward, Packages, and furthers**
New History's A.R Transmitter -> Replaced as Parable's A.R Transmitter.
