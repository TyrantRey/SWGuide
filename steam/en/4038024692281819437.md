# [Notice] 210722 Update Note Preview

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/7adcdb549abfe51a1ebfc5934a039625bb2fb588.png)

**Hello, this is an announcement from SoulWorker.
Information about 2021 July 22nd Updates taking place can be found below. **

**--------------------------------------------------◆ Updates ◆-----------------------------------------------**

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/6f82f7757f92a51ab87fd24c53d3bad3da04dfab.png)

**1. New Character Release: Lee Nabi**
- Lee Nabi, the 9th SoulWorker, will be newly added.
> Lee Nabi is the 2nd generation of SoulWorker, commanded by Lords, the supreme institution of Starry Forest League.
> Lee Nabi will begin as Level 45 after completing the tutorial.
> Her scenarios will be mainly carried out in the newly released area called ‘Tower of Lord’ and ‘Rest Room’ until Level 55.
> Lee Nabi features a long-range playstyle with Sniper Rifle.

- Advancement: Lee Nabi
> Lee Nabi’s advancement can be achieved after completing the Quest  in Level 57.
> You can receive the Quest from NPC  and obtain the advancement title after completion.
☞  You can learn extra skills for advancement but no costumes or illustrations will be given.

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/24256aef16bf409dcb14d577e433d532b9c11e27.jpg)

- Desire Awakening: Lee Nabi
> When completing the Desire Awakening, you can use her new burning skills including ‘Last Strike’, ‘DestroNail’, and ‘Tactical Airstrike’ which is accompanied with enhanced motion quality and increased Damage by 130%.
> Desire Worker Costume for Lee Nabi will be newly added.

- A new district only accessible for Lee Nabi will be newly added: ‘Tower of Lord’ and ‘Rest Room’ as a tutorial maze.
> Functions of Warp, World Boss Raid, and My Room will not be available in the district above.

- Quest for Lee Nabi will be newly added.
> Main Quests for Lee Nabi will be newly added.
+ (Main Quests and her scenarios will last until level 72 as same as the other playable characters)
+ Cinema Talks for Lee Nabi will be newly added.
> Lee Nabi players can proceed with the part of the sub-quest that the other playable characters have done.

- Rankings for Lee Nabi will be newly added.
- Lee Nabi Costumes will be newly added to the Supply Box and that of High grade for Grasscover Camp.
- Some Lee Nabi skills will be banned in the  and .
- Lee Nabi equipment series will be newly added from the primary to Twilight.
- Costumes / Photos / Appearance items for Lee Nabi will be newly added.
