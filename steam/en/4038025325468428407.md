# [Notice] In-game issues regarding Mazes in particular

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/7adcdb549abfe51a1ebfc5934a039625bb2fb588.png)

Hello, this is an announcement from SoulWorker.

We are here to inform you of in-game errors in specific mazes. Modifications are currently carried out to fix such issues that occurred after the 2nd maintenances in July, and please be notified that these would be fixed after the 1st Update in August.

Details are as follows:

** **
-	Dead Meat Factory EP. 4
-	Dawn of Fate EP. 4
-	Resurrection Project EP. 4
-	Golden Citadel (Portal deactivation for each sector)
Quests can be cleared, but the Return Portal has been deactivated after Maze Completion mentioned above.

**  **
-	Escape from the Maze to click the menu

** **
-	Gruton Coins x1000, Energy Converter x1 Super-High Dose Vitamin Z x1
-       Rewards will be distributed to the  tab of all users’ H.E.M.S after the 1st Maintenance on August

We sincerely apologize for this inconvenience, and SoulWorker will try to provide a better environment for your smooth gameplay.

Thank you.
