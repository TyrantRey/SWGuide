# [Notice] 2022 January Update Preview

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/6175be722a1c34cc5772f361753890c817e9e5c9.png)

**◆ 2022 January Update Preview**

**☞ January First Update**

**▶ EQUALIZER IMPROVEMENT: SEPARATION OF VOICE / SKILL EFFECT**
- When using an Equalizer, Voice and Skill Effect can be selected with different themes. (e.g. Voice - Theme A / Skill Effect - Theme B)

**▶ VIOLENT SUN - ADDITIONAL REWARDS**
- Additional New Drops
 > (When clear Phase 3 of Violent Sun) Dancing Scarlet Butterfly / Ivory Mayhem Usher costume have a chance to obtain with a low drop rate.
- Additional New Crafting Lists
 > NPC  of Grace City: Can craft Dancing Scarlet Butterfly / Ivory Mayhem Usher / Re-Tag Chip

**▶ NEW PREMIUM COSTUME – ‘SHINING STAR ON STAGE’**

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/6487d7d4d8ab0c80196dc2bb6fd59c1182f30b91.png)
- 'Shining Star on Stage' costume with the concept of pop idol singers will be added.
- 'Shining Star on Stage' costume includes an Equalizer.

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/9235f5c4b725bbf26e5f9243f851b8181ab2ab42.png)
- 'Serenus Spica', the stage-style district for this costume concept, will also be newly added.

**▶ NEW MEMORIAL FOR GRASSCOVER CAMP**
- A new memorial for Grasscover Camp will be added.
- The reward for the memorial will be a new accessory costume. Also enable to experience a sub-story.

**☞ January Second Update**

**▶ NEW SYSTEM: EQUIPMENT SUCCESSION (WEAPON / GEAR)**
- Can implant some of upgrade stats and random options in the equipment into the upper equipment.
- Equipment Succession requires 'Succession Device' item which can be crafted from NPC .
- Succession of Upgrade Stats: the upgrade stat value will be slightly reduced when it is implanted.
 > 'Upgrade-Protection Memory' item can alleviate decreasing upgrade stat value.
- Succession of Random Options: Can implant only one option. However, the option's stat value will be randomly determined.
 > 'Add-Option Memory' item allows you to implant up to two options.
- 'Upgrade-Protection Memory' / 'Add-Option Memory': Can purchase at Cash Shop

**▶ SOUL PASS – SEASON 2**
- A new season of Soul Pass will begin.
- It will be conducted only for a limited period of time.

**▶ CONVENIENCE IMPROVEMENT**
- The maximum number of stacks for some consumable/functional items will be increased.

**▶ LUNAR NEW YEAR EVENT**
- In celebration of the Lunar New Year, there will be events with various rewards.

☞ February Update Preview

**▶ GRAND NEW STORY – WEST WAR 3**
- The new scenario ’West War 3’ will be added. New Mazes and Monsters will be updated.
- The max level cap will be expanded.

Please look forward to our Upcoming January Update.
Happy New Year and thank you so much!

※ The update in detail may be altered due to unprecedented internal issues.
