**▶ CHANGE – Ranking System**
- Solo Maze Rankings will be separated 'by character' (Main Character of Users).
> Total Points Ranking: It will be counted by character based on the Main Character of Users.

- Ranking Mazes: Score System and Tie-Breaker Process will be changed.
> All Ranking Maze scores are equally distributed 'by Rank' and 'by Top(%)'.
> In the case of tying in the final points, users with a higher rank in the specified Ranking Maze will be ranked high.

- Ranking Reward: It will be changed to ' Gruton Medal'.
>  Gruton Medal: Can be exchanged to useful items at Gruton Coin Shop.

- New Solo Ranking Maze will be updated.
> Based on Broken Savior

- Abyss Lounge Ranking / Team Maze Ranking will be DELETED.

**▶ NEW SYSTEM – Active Skill Auto-Learn / Mastery System**
- Active Skills will be automatically learned when players reach a certain level.
 > SoulPlus of Active Skill: Enable to 'Learn' / 'Change' / 'Cancel' in the Skill tab. (No Skill Reset item is required.)

- Mastery: Consume SP to upgrade Character Stats. (Battle Mastery / Technical Mastery)

- SP requirement for Mastery / Passive Skill: It will be fixed to 'Consume SP 1' regardless of level.
