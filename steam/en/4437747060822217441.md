**▶ BROKEN SAVIOR –  MODE**
-  Mode of Broken Savior in which you can see Tenebris immersed in powerful Desire Energy will be added.

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/1c92dcbd6fa80f63c22eb93e750875bb08411bbb.png)

****
- Can enter through NPC  in Grace City.
- Solo Play and 2 ~ 4 Players Party Play are available.
- Not possible to enter with Force status.

****

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/ec5a6458888a685cc2f17fb763134192dd45584c.jpg)

- Broken Savior doesn't have any Entry Limit. (e.g. If a player has enough FP, No limit to enter the raid.)
- However, Broken Savior has a "Valid Entry" limit.
- Valid Entry: The actual number that a player can obtain Drop Items if a player clears the raid.
> Fail to clear the Raid within Valid Entry Limit → No Reward and Lose 1 chance of Valid Entry Limit
> Leaving in the middle of the Raid → No Reward and Lose 1 chance of Valid Entry Limit
- Valid Entry Limit will be Reset Weekly. (Account Base)
- When a player enters the raid → the Valid Entry Limit will be immediately deducted.
> 2 ~ 4 Players Party Play: Drop Item Rewards are only available for players with Valid Entries remaining.
> (e.g.) 3 players - A, B, C players
A - Valid Entry: 3 (Can obtain rewards) / B - Valid Entry: 0 (No rewards) / C - Valid Entry: 1 (Can obtain rewards)
- Valid Entry Limit will be "Reset Mondays at 01:00 (GMT)".

****
- Required "Accuracy": 2,500 or More
- Respawn Limit:
> Free Respawn - [3] +2 (PLUS)
> Respawn (with Items) - [3]
> Cash Respawn -

- Not possible to use ACTIVE Akashic Record
- 'Achievement' and 'Title' will be added.
- 2 Repeat Quests for Broken Savior Hard will be added.
> Repeat Quest: Can purchase at  in Grace City
