**☞ April First Update**

**☞ April Second Update**

**▶ GRAND NEW RAID – **

-Synopsis-
*I saw the light the day I was captured by the false illusion of justice.

This light put a stop to what I thought to be right.
This light of awakening made the ignorant hero inside rising once again.

The general justice I thought to be right crumbled that day along with her bitter smile.

Was my definition of justice insignificant? No, it's because her powers grew stronger.
Power was the only thing with the right of justifying justice.
That's why today... here... I will raise my sword to put into action the reality of the justice I have come to know.

This place has long been abandoned, once burning with my body due to Rosca's fierce light.
This place stands in remembrance of the mundane victory of Starry Forest League.

I stand here once more as I did before with my head held high in a different direction.*

- Season 1 Finale: 'Broken Savior' Raid will be updated.
- Above Lv. 76 can enter.
- Can obtain 'Picaresque' Weapon / Gear.

- Broken Savior: Normal / Hard level
> Can only play Normal level after the update.
> Hard level: It will be updated in May, 2022.

- About Normal level:
> Raid Entry has No Limit. However, there is Reward Limit.
> Reward Limit: The number of times that players can obtain the actual reward.
> Reward Limit is 20 per week.
> When a player enters the raid → the Reward Limit will be immediately deducted.
> Reward Condition: Must Clear the Raid within Reward Limit (20).
> Fail to clear the Raid within Reward Limit → No Reward and Lose 1 chance of Reward Limit
> Leaving in the middle of the Raid → No Reward and Lose 1 chance of Reward Limit

- Total Phase: 2
> Must clear Phase 1 within a fixed time to enter Phase 2.
- A reward can be changed according to the Phase that is cleared.

Please look forward to our Upcoming April Update.
Hope this April is full of sunshine, rainbows and beautiful sunny days.
Thank you so much!
