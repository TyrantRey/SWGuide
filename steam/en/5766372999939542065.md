# [Notice] Labor Day Maze Mayhem Event

![](https://clan.akamai.steamstatic.com/images/38551250/fd0d0d73c6ff0fb2f584bc0f519dad1bcb7c00fa.png)

Hello! This is an announcement from SoulWorker.

Join us as we celebrate Labor Day this whole week!

Mark your calendars! Be ready to embark on thrilling quests, and engage in fierce battles across different mazes. Whether you're a seasoned warrior or a newcomer to the fray, there's something for everyone in this special event.

Event Period:
May 1st 10:00 ~ May 6th 09:59 GMT
May 1st 03:00 ~ May 6th 02:59 PST
May 1st 19:00 ~ May 6th 18:59 KST
May 1st 18:00 ~ May 6th 17:59 GMT+8

- Users must run and finish the specific mazes based on the schedule below.
- All server users will get the reward once the total of clear has been reached.
- Take note that the total of maze clears is from Tenebris and Rosca servers so everyone's cooperation is greatly appreciated.

Special Reward:
If all goals are reached – users will get the following:
- Nabi's Birthday Title Package
-  Nabi's Another Beautiful Holiday

Note: Rewards will be given on May 9, 2024.

Get ready to immerse yourself in the magic of SoulWorker this Labor Day!

See you in the game!
